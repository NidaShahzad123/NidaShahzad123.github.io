Meet the villains

Spider-Man faces a variety of villains in his mission to protect Marvel's New York City,
ranging from a crime boss harnessing negative energy to a nimble thief with futuristic gadgets.

Otto Octavius

One of the primary antagonists in the 2018 video game "Marvel's Spider-Man"
and a supporting character in its 2020 spin-off "Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles
Morales" is known as the main villain.

Otto Octavius, a brilliant scientist, co-founded Oscorp but left due to ethical issues. He
formed Octavius Industries and mentors Peter Parker while working on advanced prosthetic
limbs to aid his own neurological disorder.

Scorpion

In the game Marvel's Spider-Man, Scorpion is a formidable antagonist who wears a
mechanical suit featuring a massive stinging tail. Alongside the other members of the
Sinister Six, Scorpion manages to escape from the high-security prison called The Raft.

Rhino

Rhino possesses incredible speed despite his massive size, enabling him to run at high
velocities. This remarkable agility allows him to inflict significant damage to both his
enemies and the surrounding environment through charging or ramming.

Electro

Among Spider-Man's adversaries, Electro stands out as the most carefree, often engaging in
banter with the web-slinger during their encounters. However, he can also come across as
arrogant and hyperactive on occasion. Electro's primary ambition is to transform into "pure
energy," driving his ultimate objective.

Shocker

Shocker is a formidable antagonist armed with an advanced battle suit that empowers him
to unleash concentrated shockwaves of concussive force through his gauntlets. It's
important to note that Shocker's abilities should not be confused with those of Electro, who
harnesses the power of electricity.

Vulture

In Marvel's Spider-Man, Adrian Toomes, also known as Vulture, plays a supporting
antagonistic role. As a former electronics engineer, he utilizes advanced artificial wings that
enable him to soar at incredible speeds. Vulture's distinctive appearance includes a beak-
shaped oxygen mask, granting him a robotic voice.

Kingpin

Wilson Fisk, aka the Kingpin, is a major antagonist in Marvel's Spider-Man. He's a powerful
crime boss controlling New York City's underworld. Despite his early defeat, Fisk's influence
sparks chaos as rival villains compete for control. In Spider-Man: Miles Morales, he attempts
to regain power but is ultimately apprehended and faces an extended prison sentence.

Mr.Negative

Mister Negative possesses the power to generate and manipulate a potent electric negative
energy. He utilizes this energy to unleash devastating blasts from his hands and infuse it into
his swords, merging his dark abilities with his exceptional swordsmanship during combat.

Taskmaster

Taskmaster is a minor antagonist in Marvel's Spider-Man, a well-funded mercenary
shrouded in mystery. His unique ability to replicate the physical movements of others makes
him a formidable adversary in combat.
